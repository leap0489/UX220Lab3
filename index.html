The Best Movie I Watched Recently

Your Name; My Personal Favorite

The best movie I watched recently was Your Name. If I had to guess, I think this most recent time I watched this movie was the the tenth time I have seen it. I came across this movie when I was in a pretty rough mental state in highschool and it was something that genuinely brought me joy at the time. Surprisingly, things actually got quite better in my life a bit after watching this movie so whenever I re-watch the movie I think back to those good times.

What I Enjoyed about Your Name

The two main things I enjoyed about Your Name were its unpredictability and romance. I found that the latter half of the movie had a good, but unexpected twist which truly left me questioning what was going to happen in every next part. I also really enjoyed the chemistry between the main characters because after all, I am just a guy who loves love.

Your Name Soundtrack

Another great aspect of the movie which I think is worth mentioning is the music. I found that the soundtrack did a great job of mixing cinematic orchestra with rock which are two genres I find interesting. For the past few years, I have even used the soundtrack as study music.
